All refrigerant piping shall be tested per the following: Pressure Testing for leaks • Charge system with regulated dry nitrogen and the appropriate tracer gas to pressure defined by engineer of record (EOR) or Manufacturer. • Branches may be tested in segments to reduce the time needed to locate leaks.
