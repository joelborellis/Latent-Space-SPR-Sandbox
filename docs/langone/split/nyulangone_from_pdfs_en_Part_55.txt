Sustainability & Healthcare DatabaseHealth and environmental sustainability are fundamentally connected and are essential for the livelihood of our communities. NYU Langone and NYU researchers have recognized this connection and have led by example through their academic studies on this topic. This Sustainability & Healthcare Database compiles their publications on the intersection of public health, sustainability, pollution, and climate change. This database serves as an educational resource that illustrates how hospitals can demonstrate leadership and allow research to drive meaningful changes in practice and care.Download the Sustainability & Healthcare Database here.This document shows the database organized by categories.
